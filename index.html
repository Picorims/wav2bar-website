Wav2Bar

The free, open source audio visualization creator.

Wav2Bar lets you make nice visuals for your audio source. You can then export it as a video to share on YouTube, your social medias, your website and more.

Free

You will never have to pay to use Wav2Bar. Your projects remains yours.

Open Source

Wav2Bar is MIT licensed. That means you can browse the source code on GitHub, and modify it locally.

Customization

Every element has customization options to let you make something that suits you the most. One is missing ? Request it!

Video Export

Use whatever resolution you like, and export is as .mp4 for your website or your social medias.

All kinds of features exists to make unique videos.

  • Save your projects, and use them as templates;
  • Multiple audio visualizer types (More to come);
  • Custom resolution and framerate;
  • Time indicators;
  • Images, like your own brand logo;
  • Particle flows;
  • And more...
